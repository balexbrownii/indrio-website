### What's in Your Library

**USDA Standards Problem (TED Talk content):**
> "USDA grades citrus by peel color, not sugar content. Grocers pay by the pound, not the brix. Farmers are forced to pick unripe so fruit survives a 2,000-mile cold chain."

**California Citrus Maturity Formula:**
```
Maturity Score = (Brix - (4 × Titratable Acidity)) × 16.5
```
A score of 90+ indicates harvest readiness. This is actual science!
