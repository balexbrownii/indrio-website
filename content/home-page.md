## SECTION 2: THE PROBLEM

### Headline
You're gambling every time you buy groceries.

### Body Copy
Walk into any grocery store. Pick up an orange. What do you know about it?

You don't know when it was picked. You don't know how sweet it is. You don't know if it was bred for flavor or for surviving a cross-country truck ride.

The grocery store doesn't know either.

**That's the gamble.** Sometimes you get lucky. Sometimes you don't. The house always wins—you keep coming back hoping this time will be different.

**We offer certainty.**

Every piece of fruit we ship meets a quality floor. Measurable. Verifiable. Guaranteed.

No more gambling.

## SECTION 4: THE S.H.A.R.E. FRAMEWORK

### Headline
Our quality standard

### Intro
Five principles. Every product. No exceptions.

### The 5 Pillars (Icon + Title + Description for each)

**S — SOIL**
Healthy soil builds the foundation. Nutrient-rich earth grows nutrient-rich food. We work with growers who invest in their land, not just extract from it.

**H — HERITAGE**
Varieties selected for flavor and nutrition, not shipping durability. The breeds and cultivars that taste like food used to taste—before industrial agriculture optimized for yield.

**A — AGRICULTURAL**
Practices that minimize synthetic inputs. Not because "natural" is magic, but because healthy ecosystems produce better food. Science over marketing.

**R — RIPENING**
Harvested at the right moment. Tree-ripened citrus. Grass-finished beef. Not picked green and gassed in a warehouse to fake ripeness.

**E — ENRICHING**
Benefits the entire ecosystem—from the microbes in the soil to the families who eat the food. Fair prices for farmers. Quality for customers. Sustainable for the land.
